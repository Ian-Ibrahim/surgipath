About us

Surgipath Services E.A. Ltd is a leading service provider for medical diagnostic and laboratory needs. We take pride in providing efficient, accessible, quality and affordable services, across 47 counties in Kenya and other East Africa countries.
Established in 2004, Surgipath Services E.A. Ltd is an ISO Certified company distinguished by her corporate values. Surgipath has a unique distribution and marketing network of 25 medical sales representatives and medical engineers. The company is managed by highly experience directors with diverse backgrounds and skill set.

Mission

Surgipath Services E.A Ltd. is dedicated to market quality diagnostics and medical products and services to the healthcare institutions reasonably and ensure that the customers will come and continue to turn to Surgipath Services E.A Ltd.

Vision

To become the preferred diagnostics Company in East Africa

Goal

Our long term goal is to become the largest & preferred diagnostic and laboratory product distributor in the East African market

Quality statement

We are committed to providing the highest quality products and services that meet and even exceed the requirements of our customers within the confines of the law.
In pursuit of this commitment, we have established a Quality Management System to

  • Continually improve the performance of the Quality Management System (QMS) by building Surgipath Services E.A. Ltd institutional capacities
  • Establish, implement, and review quality objectives regularly
  • Communicate the importance of meeting our customer's requirements and the requirements of the QMS to all employees
  • Engage highly skilled human resource
  • Use effective communication internally and externally
