About us

Surgipath Services (EA) Limited is a company that provides medical diagnostic and general laboratory services in the East Africa region. It started operations in 2004 with a share capital of Kshs. 2 million and a small workforce of four people, but has since grown to have a workforce of thirty employees. The company values the quality of its products and services and considers it to be its core value and competitive edge in the market

Mission

To provide quality diagnostics products and service to healthcare institutions to meet their requirements cost effectively

Vision

To become the preferred diagnostics Company in East Africa

Goal

Our long-term goal is to become the largest & preferred diagnostic company in the East African market and also extend the business to the Democratic republic of Congo (DRC), Ethiopia, Rwanda, Burundi and Eritrea
